Osden Jokonya

Dr Osden Jokonya is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Information Systems at the North-West University. Osden has managed IT projects in various industries, including mining, retail, manufacturing, financial services, insurance, utilities, education and other sectors. He has presented papers at international conferences in addition to publishing in several journals. He is a committee member of a number of international conferences and editor of international journals.

His current research interest focuses on digital transformation, financial technologies and SMAC stack (Social media, Mobility, Big Data Analytics and Cloud).
