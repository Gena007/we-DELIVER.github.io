The Project

The we-DELIVER project includes a wide variety of researchers from several research areas as well as students at various levels (undergraduates, postgraduates, as well as postdoctoral research fellows). Project members may only be involved at certain stages depending on their role and expertise. We wanted to create a project where most data (where ethically possible) and information about the process, progress, and other aspects were easily accessible throughout the course of the project rather than a few years later when it's finally published. That would allow project members to spend their time contributing their skills and expertise rather than be caught up in emails or meetings to understand how they can get involved or commence with their work.

We decided to use the following tools to enable a collaborative environment for a team with diverse research backgrounds and skill sets who are also geographically dispersed:

Tool Purpose Link (some services may only be available to project team members)
GitHub Free hosting of our project website that will allow any project member or other interested party to contribute to the site https://we-deliver.github.io
Google Drive Hosting of project documentation, collaborative document editing Not publicly available - only open to project members
Vidyo Video conferencing, training (the software is supported by TENET) Request a Vidyo account
Trello Project management, communication Trello board (only project members)
Sutori Story telling, project timeline we-DELIVER Sutori
YouTube Story telling, providing information, training we-DELIVER YouTube Channel (will follow shortly)
Nextcloud Research data sharing amongst project members (hosted by NWU IT) Raw data only accesible to project members until de-identified and cleaned
