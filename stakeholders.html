Three Teams - One University

The three teams of the we-DELIVER project include researchers, students, and support staff from Mafikeng, Vaal Triangle, and Potchefstroom. The we-DELIVER project applies a snowball recruiting method. This organic approach leaves it open for members to join the project in different phases or in terms of their specific interests or subject expertise.
